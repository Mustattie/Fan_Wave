Fan Sphere Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account (email, display name, home city), content you post (messages, clips, moments), and usage data (app interactions, device info).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: provide the Fan Sphere service, personalize your experience (local groups/parties), send notifications you've opted into, and improve the app.

3. Location Data

We use your city preference (not precise GPS) to show nearby watch parties and fan groups. Location is never shared with other users without your consent.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with: Supabase (infrastructure), Expo (push notifications), and as required by law.

5. Your Content

Messages, clips, and moments you post are visible to group members. Public groups are visible to all authenticated users. You can delete your own content at any time.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. You can request account deletion by contacting support@fansphere.org.

7. Children's Privacy

Fan Sphere is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We'll notify you of significant changes via the app.

9. Contact Us

Questions? Email us at privacy@fansphere.org
